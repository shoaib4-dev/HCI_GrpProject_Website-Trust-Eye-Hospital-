About Trust Eye Hospital

Established in 1998, Trust Eye Hospital has been a pioneer in providing affordable and quality eye care services. With a special focus on visually impaired patients, we combine modern technology with compassionate care to restore hope and independence.

Our Mission

Our mission is to eliminate preventable blindness through early diagnosis, advanced treatment, and rehabilitation programs. We believe every person deserves the gift of sight, regardless of their financial background.

Vision: is not just about eyesight, but about quality of life. That is why we extend our services to rural and underserved areas.
